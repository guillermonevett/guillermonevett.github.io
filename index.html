Welcome

I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering at the University of Colorado Boulder. My dissertation is focused on productivity in highway construction projects. Specifically, I'm studying the impact of bid quantities and project characteristics on the duration of construction of road transportation projects, under Dr. Paul Goodrum's supervision.
